About the Shelter

We are a team of volunteers for whom rescuing homeless dogs has become a life's work. Our shelter in Parekklisia village near Limassol is not just a temporary place, it's a home where every dog receives love and care.

Every day we feed, treat, and socialize our residents, preparing them to meet their new families. We believe that every dog deserves a second chance at a happy life.

The shelter exists thanks to the support of caring people and companies. Every donation, every hour of volunteer work brings our dogs closer to their dream of finding a loving home.

From Shelter to Home

Volunteer charity program

Step 01

Choose a Dog

Browse the catalog and sponsor one or more dogs. Become their temporary mentor and friend on the way to a new home.

Step 02

Spend Time Together

Visit the shelter 1-2 times a week for walks, games and socialization. Your interaction helps dogs adapt to family life.

Step 03

Tell Their Story

Share photos and videos of your sponsored dog on social media. Talk about their character, habits and dreams. Your voice will help find the perfect family.

Step 04

Help Find a Home

Use your network to find a loving family for your dog. Become the link between the shelter and new owners.
